Some Useful Data

  • UN Comtrade
    • The most comprehensive global trade flows database maintained by the UN Statistics Division.
    • Annual data for ~200 economies since 1962; HS6 product granularity.
  • WITS (World Integrated Trade Solution)
    • Unified interface aggregating UN Comtrade, UNCTAD, and WTO tariff data.
    • Particularly strong for tariff analysis: effectively applied, MFN, and bound tariff rates at HS6.
  • WTO Statistics
    • Authoritative WTO source for tariffs: bound, MFN, and applied rates for all WTO members.
    • Also covers trade in services and global trade forecasts.
  • TiVA-OECD
    • Value-added decomposition of bilateral trade for 80 economies; coverage 1995–2022.
    • Indicators: domestic value-added share in gross exports, foreign value-added content, services value-added in trade, and others.
